704 Tennis is a small business that provides tennis instruction for all levels of players from young to old. We provide tennis instruction in an atmosphere that can not be beat. Currently 704 Tennis is working with the town of Harrisburg, Davidson and Mooresville.
 
Meet your instructor:

Coach Neil

Coach Neil has nearly twelve years of experience coaching both children and adults in the game of tennis. His coaching career began in the suburbs of Chicago, working for the Town of Wilmette; when he moved to the Charlotte area in 2008, he began teaching tennis lessons for Harrisburg Parks and Recreation, and is now in his sixth year coaching on the Stallings Road Park courts. Coach Neil has attended both Bollettieri and Van Der Meer tennis academies; his teaching style has been influenced by those experiences. Coach Neil has also completed Tennis University at Van Der Meer Academy and is a PTR certified instructor. When not on the court, he enjoys spending time with his family (attempting to keep up his 3 boys), cheering for the Chicago Cubs, and sipping ice coffee.
